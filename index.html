Ada Lovelace

The first computer programmer

Time line of Ada Lovelace's life:

  • 1815 - Born on the 10th of December in London, England
  • 1829 - Paralyzed after contracting measles and subjected to continuous bed rest for almost a year
  • 1831 - Able to walk again with crutches and despite her illnesses, she developed her technological and mathematical skills
  • 1852 - Died aged 36 on the 27th of November in Marylebone, London, England

If you are interested, you can find out more about Ada in her wikipedia entry.
